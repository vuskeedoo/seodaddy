What is Search Engine Optization (SEO)?

Search engine optimization (SEO) is the practice of increasing the quantity and quality of traffic to your website through organic search engine results. To understand what SEO really means, let's break that sentence down and look at the parts:

  • Quality of traffic. You can attract all the visitors in the world, but if they're coming to your site because Google tells them you're a resource for Apple computers when really you're a farmer selling apples, that is not quality traffic. Instead you want to attract visitors who are genuinely interested in products that you offer.
  • Quantity of traffic. Once you have the right people clicking through from those search engine results pages (SERPs), more traffic is better.
  • Organic results. Ads make up a significant portion of many SERPs. Organic traffic is any traffic that you don't have to pay for.

What countries and locations are available?

Is data provided in real-time?
